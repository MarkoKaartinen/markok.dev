Let's start again with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/). First iteration of MarkoK.dev was a custom-made Laravel app. Second was a Discourse installation. Now let's try Hugo.

My .dev is supposed to be a playground — a place for me to test stuff and do something stupid/cool.

Discourse is a great platform for discussions, but for blogging I prefer Hugo, WordPress, or even my own apps. It was a great learning experience, but just not the right fit for this use case.

I didn't want to bring back the old MarkoK.dev with Laravel either, since my [MarkoKaartinen.net](https://markokaartinen.net) already runs on a custom Laravel app — and I wanted to keep the two separate. Hugo is a great fit for doing something like this, lightweight and fun to use.

This blog is my place to write about whatever I want, in English. The goal is to keep it easy, light, and simple - at least the writing part.

This is a bit of a mess for a blog post. Get used to it 😆

Commenting is handled by [giscus](https://giscus.app/), which is basically a GitHub-powered discussion system. The blog is also fully public on [GitHub](https://github.com/MarkoKaartinen/markok.dev).

This is the first iteration of this Hugo-based blog. More stuff is coming — hopefully some fun stuff too.

Alright, I'm done rambling. See you next time.
